What is the Online Photobooth?

Our Online Photobooth is a browser-based tool that lets you capture photos instantly without installing any apps. You can take pictures directly from your device, customize them with frames, filters, and stickers, and download the final result within seconds. It’s simple, fast, and made for effortless creativity.

How It Works

Follow these quick steps to use the photobooth:

  1. Open the Booth page
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Choose your filter or frame
  4. Take your photo
  5. Customize it in the editor (stickers, text, colors, layouts)
  6. Download or share your final photobooth strip

Everything happens right inside your browser — no sign-in, no app, no hassle.

Privacy & Safety

  • Your photos are never stored by the admin
  • No automatic uploads happen in the background
  • Works fully inside your browser (local processing)
  • You choose what to save or delete

Your photos belong only to you — always.

Additional Information

  • Fully accessible on mobile devices
  • Requires camera permission to function
  • Lightweight and fast to load
  • Works completely without installing any app

Perfect for quick selfies, fun moments, or creative photobooth strips anytime, anywhere.
